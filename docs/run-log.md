## Run #1

- **Date:** 2026-04-07
- **Character:** 铁甲战士 (Ironclad)
- **Result:** ❌ Defeated at Act 1 Boss (Floor 17)
- **Ascension:** 0
- **Boss:** 墨影幻灵 (Vantom) — 173 HP, 9 layers 滑溜, gains Strength

### Deck (17 cards)
- 打击 ×4, 防御 ×4, 痛击+ (2), 血墙 (2), 燃烧+ (1), 双重打击 (1), 拆卸 (1), 耸肩无视 (1), 薪火之源 (2), 暴走 (1), 狂怒 (0)

### Potions
- 力量药水 (used T1 of boss fight)

### Death Cause
Vantom's 滑溜 (9 layers) absorbed all early damage — each hit only dealt 1 damage. By the time 滑溜 was stripped (Round 7), the boss had +4 Strength and was dealing 29+ per turn. Meanwhile, 6+ 伤口 (Wound) status cards clogged the deck, causing dead draws at critical lethal-threat moments. Final turn: hand was 1 Strike + 1 狂怒 + 3 Wounds vs 20 incoming damage. Only generated 3 block — died.

1. **Elite #1 (Byrdonis, Floor 7):** Clean win. Got 金刚杵 (+1 Str/combat) and 薪火之源 (Rare, +1 energy/turn). Huge power spike.
2. **Elite #2 (Phrog Parasite, Floor 9):** Won but took heavy Infection damage. Got 精致折扇 (+4 block per 3 attacks). Good relic but Infections polluted deck.
3. **Boss (Vantom, Floor 17):** 滑溜 mechanic was devastating. Spent 6 turns just stripping the buff, while boss scaled Strength and flooded deck with Wounds.

### What Went Right
- Two elite fights for 2 relics — excellent value
- 燃烧+ upgrade was correct priority
- 薪火之源 + 燃烧+ = +6 Str and 4 energy engine
- Skipped bad card rewards to keep deck lean (17 cards)

### What Went Wrong
- **Didn't play 薪火之源 on Turn 1** — played it on Turn 4 after already spending rounds at 3 energy
- **No exhaust/purge mechanic** to deal with Wound clog from boss
- **Insufficient multi-hit cards** for stripping 滑溜 — only Twin Strike and Dismantle hit multiple times
- **Prioritized single-target damage over utility** — should have taken AOE or card draw to handle status cards
- **Used Fire Potion on Inklets** (low-threat fight) instead of saving for boss
