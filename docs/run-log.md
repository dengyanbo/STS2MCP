# STS2 MCP — Run Log

## Run #1

- **Date:** 2026-04-07
- **Character:** 铁甲战士 (Ironclad)
- **Result:** ❌ Defeated at Act 1 Boss (Floor 17)
- **Ascension:** 0
- **Boss:** 墨影幻灵 (Vantom) — 173 HP, 9 layers 滑溜, gains Strength

### Deck (17 cards)
- 打击 ×4, 防御 ×4, 痛击+ (2), 血墙 (2), 燃烧+ (1), 双重打击 (1), 拆卸 (1), 耸肩无视 (1), 薪火之源 (2), 暴走 (1), 狂怒 (0)

### Relics
- 燃烧之血, 金刚杵, 精致折扇, 护喉甲

### Potions
- 力量药水 (used T1 of boss fight)

### Death Cause
Vantom's 滑溜 (9 layers) absorbed all early damage — each hit only dealt 1 damage. By the time 滑溜 was stripped (Round 7), the boss had +4 Strength and was dealing 29+ per turn. Meanwhile, 6+ 伤口 (Wound) status cards clogged the deck, causing dead draws at critical lethal-threat moments. Final turn: hand was 1 Strike + 1 狂怒 + 3 Wounds vs 20 incoming damage. Only generated 3 block — died.

### Key Moments
1. **Elite #1 (Byrdonis, Floor 7):** Clean win. Got 金刚杵 (+1 Str/combat) and 薪火之源 (Rare, +1 energy/turn). Huge power spike.
2. **Elite #2 (Phrog Parasite, Floor 9):** Won but took heavy Infection damage. Got 精致折扇 (+4 block per 3 attacks). Good relic but Infections polluted deck.
3. **Boss (Vantom, Floor 17):** 滑溜 mechanic was devastating. Spent 6 turns just stripping the buff, while boss scaled Strength and flooded deck with Wounds.

### What Went Right
- Two elite fights for 2 relics — excellent value
- 燃烧+ upgrade was correct priority
- 薪火之源 + 燃烧+ = +6 Str and 4 energy engine
- Skipped bad card rewards to keep deck lean (17 cards)

### What Went Wrong
- **Didn't play 薪火之源 on Turn 1** — played it on Turn 4 after already spending rounds at 3 energy
- **No exhaust/purge mechanic** to deal with Wound clog from boss
- **Insufficient multi-hit cards** for stripping 滑溜 — only Twin Strike and Dismantle hit multiple times
- **Prioritized single-target damage over utility** — should have taken AOE or card draw to handle status cards
- **Used Fire Potion on Inklets** (low-threat fight) instead of saving for boss

## Run #2

- **Date:** 2026-04-08
- **Character:** 铁甲战士 (Ironclad)
- **Result:** ❌ Defeated at Act 1 Elite (Floor 15)
- **Ascension:** 0
- **Elite:** 旧日雕像 (Bygone Effigy) — 127 HP, Slow debuff, +10 Strength from buffs

### Deck (13 cards)
- 打击 ×3, 防御 ×4, 痛击+ (2), 放血 (0), 无情猛攻+ (2), 劫掠 (1), 势不可当+ (2), 挑衅 (1), 武装 (1)

### Relics
- 燃烧之血, 失物盒, 药水腰带

### Potions
- 镣铐药水 (used on elite Round 4), 迅捷药水 (used on elite Round 1)

### Death Cause
旧日雕像 gained 10 Strength from its buff turns, dealing 25 damage/turn. Despite 势不可当+ providing passive damage via block triggers (7 damage per block gain), couldn't kill the 127 HP elite fast enough. Entered the elite at 63 HP which proved insufficient. Final turn: 13 HP, 25 incoming, couldn't generate 25+ block or kill 43 HP remaining.

### Key Moments
1. **Neow (Floor 1):** 失物盒 gave card reward + potion. Took 放血 (0-cost energy generation).
2. **Floor 4:** Got 势不可当 (Rare Power) — core damage engine via block gains.
3. **Floor 5 Shop:** Removed a Strike, bought 挑衅 (7 block + 1 Vuln, 势不可当 synergy).
4. **Floor 8 Slime Fight:** Got 壁垒 from 能力药水 — the combo of 壁垒+势不可当 was incredible but temporary (potion-only).
5. **Floor 9 Event:** Upgraded 势不可当 to 势不可当+ (5→7 damage per block).
6. **Floor 12 Event:** Removed another Strike for 11 HP cost.
7. **Floor 15 Elite:** 旧日雕像 buffed to 10 Str. Despite using both potions and playing optimally with Slow mechanic, couldn't outpace its damage.

### What Went Right
- 势不可当+ is a strong win condition with block-heavy decks
- Removed 2 Strikes for better deck consistency (13 cards)
- 武装 provides free upgrades while blocking (great efficiency)
- Good card discipline — skipped bad rewards

### What Went Wrong
- **Too much HP lost to slime status card clog** (Floor 8) — entered mid-game at 26 HP
- **No reliable scaling damage** — 势不可当+ does 7/block but attacks still weak
- **Entered elite at 63 HP** — should have been 80% HP (64+) for elites
- **Used 镣铐药水 on elite instead of saving for boss** — but had no choice
- **Batch combat actions unreliable** — index shifting caused wrong cards to play multiple times
